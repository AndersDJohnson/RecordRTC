new MRecordRTC()

MRecordRTC stands for "Multiple-RecordRTC".

MRecordRTC runs top over RecordRTC to bring multiple recordings in single place, by providing simple API.

Example
var recorder = new MRecordRTC();
recorder.addStream(MediaStream);
recorder.mediaType = {
    audio: true,
    video: true,
    gif: true
};
recorder.startRecording();
